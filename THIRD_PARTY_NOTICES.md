## FFmpegKit community Android build

The Android app uses
`io.github.maxrave-dev:ffmpeg-kit-audio:6.0.1` as an LGPL 3.0 FFmpegKit audio
build with Android 16 KB page-size support.

- Package source: <https://github.com/maxrave-dev/ffmpeg-kit-16KB>
- Maven Central:
  <https://central.sonatype.com/artifact/io.github.maxrave-dev/ffmpeg-kit-audio/6.0.1>
- FFmpeg: <https://ffmpeg.org/>
- FFmpeg legal and license information: <https://ffmpeg.org/legal.html>

The audio package includes LAME support for MP3 output and does not require a
GPL video bundle. Before distributing a release outside personal use, verify
the exact AAR build configuration and include the corresponding LGPL license
text and relinking/source offer materials required by that binary distribution.
